Q&A Section

Ask questions and get answers from our community members.

Question: How do I start with strength training?

Answer: Start with light weights and focus on proper form. Gradually increase weight as you build strength. A certified trainer can guide you to prevent injuries.

Question: What's a good meal plan for building muscle?

Answer: Focus on high-protein foods like chicken, fish, eggs, and legumes. Pair them with complex carbs like brown rice and vegetables to fuel your workouts and recovery.
